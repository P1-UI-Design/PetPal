Adoption Process

The adoption process begins with filling out an application form. After submission, our team will review the application to ensure the best fit between the pet and the potential owner. This can take up to 5 days. Successful applicants will be contacted for a home visit or virtual interview. Once approved, a meeting with the pet is arranged.

Fees

The adoption fee varies depending on the pet's age, size, and breed. Generally:

  • Cats: $50 - $150
  • Dogs: $100 - $300
  • Rabbits: $25 - $50
The fee includes vaccinations, microchipping, and a vet check.

Requirements

Adopters must meet the following criteria:

  • Must be at least 21 years old.
  • Provide a stable living environment.
  • Have the consent of all household members.
  • Have a valid ID with current address.
  • Agree to a home visit or virtual interview.

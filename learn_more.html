Adoption Process

The adoption process is designed to find the best match for both the pet and the adopter. The steps include:

  1. Visit our website and browse through the available pets.
  2. Choose a pet that you feel a connection with.
  3. Click on "Apply for Adoption" and fill in the required details.
  4. Wait for the shelter to review your application. They might ask further questions or arrange a meeting.
  5. If all goes well, you'll be able to bring your new pet home!

Adoption Fees

Adoption fees help cover the costs of caring for our animals. They include:

  • Dogs: $150 - Covers vaccination, microchipping, and initial vet visit.
  • Cats: $100 - Includes spaying/neutering, and first round of vaccinations.
  • Other animals: Prices vary depending on species and care requirements. Contact the shelter for specifics.
Note: Fees are subject to change and may vary based on age, breed, or special needs.

Adoption Requirements

To ensure the well-being of our animals, adopters must meet the following requirements:

  • Proof of residence (lease or utility bill).
  • Valid identification (driver's license or state ID).
  • Must be 18 years or older.
  • If renting, provide written consent from landlord.
  • Agree to a home inspection (for certain pets).
  • Provide references (vet or personal).
